Official Opening of the New Robotics Laboratory

The new laboratory is equipped with the latest devices, 3D printers and robotic kits.

We are pleased to announce that the new robotics and automation laboratory has been officially opened. The laboratory is equipped with state-of-the-art devices that will allow students to receive practical training in robotics, automation, and 3D printing.

Equipment and Opportunities

The new laboratory is equipped with: 15 Arduino and Raspberry Pi robotic kits, 3 professional 3D printers, complete IoT infrastructure and sensor systems, and computers with specialized simulation software.

Future Plans

In the coming period, the laboratory will serve as the foundation for our school’s competitive teams at regional and national levels. Collaborations with local companies for dual education are also planned.

We thank everyone who contributed to the realization of this project – parents, partners, and the local self-government.
