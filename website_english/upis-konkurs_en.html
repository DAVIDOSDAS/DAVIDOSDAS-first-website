II. Enrollment conditions and selection criteria

Enrollment of students in public secondary schools is carried out according to the legally prescribed conditions and selection criteria.

  • Completed primary education (nine years)
  • Valid school records and report cards for grades VI, VII, VIII and IX
  • Birth certificate
  • Citizenship certificate
  • Medical certificate of health status (for vocational programs)

Selection criteria for students:

  • Success in grades VI, VII, VIII and IX (average grade × 10 points)
  • Behavior grade (1–5 points per year)
  • Diplomas and awards from state competitions (1–10 points)
  • Maximum number of points: 100

Timeline for Admission

June 2025 — Competition Announcement

The Ministry of Education announces the public competition for enrollment in secondary schools.

June / July 2025 — First Enrollment Session

Submission of documents, processing of applications and publication of preliminary lists.

August 2025 — Second Enrollment Session

Enrollment in remaining places for candidates who did not enroll in the first session.

Formal opening of the 2025/2026 school year.
