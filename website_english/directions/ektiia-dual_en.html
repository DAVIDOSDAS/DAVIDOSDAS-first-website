What is dual education?

This track combines theoretical classes at school with practical work in partner companies. From the first year, students receive a salary, real work experience, and the opportunity for immediate employment upon completion.

Main advantages

  • 50–70% of the time is spent in a company (practical training)
  • Monthly salary during schooling (according to law)
  • Working with modern equipment and technologies
  • Mentorship from experienced engineers
  • Employment guarantee with the partner company (per agreement)
  • Opportunity to acquire international certificates

What will you do in the company?

Assembly, maintenance, and programming of electromechanical systems, computer networks, automation, robotics, and IoT devices.

After graduation

Direct employment in IT companies, industry, energy, automation.
Continuing to university (electrical engineering, computer science, robotics).
