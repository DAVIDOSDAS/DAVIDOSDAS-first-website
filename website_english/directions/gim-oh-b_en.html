What does this track offer?

This program provides a solid foundation for students interested in legal systems, business administration, and the global economy.

  • Introduction to law and legal procedures
  • Economic principles and business management
  • Development of entrepreneurial skills
  • Critical analysis of social and political phenomena
  • Practical training in administration and business sectors
  • Workshops on solar energy systems and sustainability

Where do we continue after graduation?

Faculties of Law, Economics, Business Administration, Management, and International Relations.
Careers in the legal profession, banking, entrepreneurship, and corporate management.
