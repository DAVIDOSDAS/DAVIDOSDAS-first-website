Computer Technique and Automation

Shaping the future through hardware engineering, industrial robotics, and intelligent control systems.

Apply for this track Back to all tracks

What does this track offer?

This four-year technical program focuses on the intersection of computer systems and automated industrial processes, bridging the gap between digital logic and physical motion.

  • Computer hardware architecture and maintenance
  • Programming and configuring PLC controllers
  • Fundamentals of robotics and automated production lines
  • Network administration and system security
  • Sensors, actuators, and industrial interfaces
  • Diagnostics and repair of electronic computer equipment

Where do we continue after graduation?

Faculties of Electrical Engineering and Information Technologies (FEIT), Computer Science and Engineering (FINKI), and Mechanical Engineering.
Careers as Automation Engineers, Hardware Technicians, System Administrators, and Robotics Specialists.
