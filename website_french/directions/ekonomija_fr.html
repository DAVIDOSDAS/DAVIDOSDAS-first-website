What does this track offer?

This track equips students for careers in economics, trade, finance, and business management. The focus is on practical knowledge, digital tools, and real-world business simulations.

  • Accounting and financial reporting
  • Marketing, digital marketing, and e-commerce
  • Business law and entrepreneurship
  • Project management and teamwork
  • Economics in the digital era and startup culture
  • Practical training in real companies (dual education opportunity)

Where do we continue after graduation?

Faculties of Economics, Business Administration, Marketing, Finance, Law, Tourism.
Direct employment in banks, trading companies, marketing agencies, startups.
